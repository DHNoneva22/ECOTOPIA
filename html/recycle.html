RECYCLE LOCATION

Recycling paper is an important practice that helps conserve natural resources, reduce waste, and
minimize environmental impact. When paper is recycled, it can be turned into new paper products, reducing the need to cut down more trees.

Recycling plastic is a process that involves collecting,
sorting, cleaning, and processing plastic waste to produce new products. This helps to reduce the
amount of plastic waste that ends up in landfills and
the environment, thus contributing to environmental sustainability.

Recycling glass is an important environmental
practice that helps to reduce waste and conserve
natural resources. Glass is 100% recyclable and
can be recycled endlessly without losing its quality
or purity.
